Save The Dragons
Here at Save The Dragons our mission is to provide aid and care for the forgotten dragons of the world. Dragons are almost extinct due to adventurers conquests. You've probable never seen a real dragon and there is a reason for that. Due to thier habitat being on the tallest and most remote mountains very few have ever seen one. additionally due to humans hunting them for hundreds of years they have chosen to stay away to avoid being hunted. Their food is very scarce so even though they aren't being actively hunted there population hasn't increased and instead remains on a steady decline.
